# BibliCal v1.1 – Production Release

**Release date:** February 12, 2026  
**Version:** 1.1 (versionCode 7)

## What's New in v1.1

- **Passages integration** – Share parsha week and month info with PassAges app
- **Production Ready** – App polished for production use
- **Working iOS version** – iOS companion app available
- **Fix notification timing** – Improved notification display and timing
- **Fix widget spacing** – Better layout for home-screen widgets

## Highlights Since v0.2.0

- Welcome screen improvements
- Jerusalem Time support
- Extra widgets (combined, date, Shabbat)
- Calendar updates and date setting fixes
- Bug fixes and stability improvements

## Features

- **Today screen** – Shows "Month X, Day Y, Year Z" based on recorded month starts
- **Projected calendar view** – Current month and future projections until you confirm actual starts
- **Persistent notification** – Optional lunar date in status bar
- **Home-screen widgets** – Today's lunar date + Gregorian date
- **Day 29/30 prompts** – Confirm new moon sighting to start next month
- **Aviv barley prompt (12/29)** – Decide new year vs 13th month
- **Device calendar export** – Export month starts and feast days to your calendar

## Installation

1. Download `app-release.apk` from the Assets below
2. Enable "Install from Unknown Sources" on your Android device
3. Install the APK

## Downloads

- **APK** – `app-release.apk` (for direct install / sideloading)
